About Roditha Hotel

Roditha Hotel is a three stars hotel, ideally situated only 25 minutes by taxi or car from Banjarmasin – Syamsudin Noor Airport and just 15 minutes from Trisakti Harbour, good location on the main road in the front of Mitra Plaza Shopping Centre.
